School Holiday Fun at The Arts Centre

In April 2026, Christchurch offers a variety of children's activities across the [Arts Centre Te Matatiki Toi Ora](https://www.artscentre.org.nz/whats-on/school-holiday-fun/), city libraries, and other community venues.

The Arts Centre hosts an extensive "School Holiday Fun" program running from April 2 to April 19, 2026.

Creative Workshops & Classes:

  • Paint an Easter Egg: A drop-in session for children (with adult supervision) on Thursday, April 2 and Sunday, April 5 at the School of Art.
  • Ōtautahi's Next Top Rocket: A bottle-rocket building and launching session on Tuesdays, April 7 and April 14.
  • Initiation to Puppetry Workshop: A Bunraku and shadow puppetry class on Friday, April 10.
  • African Drum Workshop: A group rhythm and drumming circle on Monday, April 13.

Library Activities

[Christchurch City Libraries](https://christchurch.bibliocommons.com) also offer regular programming during this time:

  • Wā Kōrero-Storytimes: For preschoolers at various locations including Tūranga and Matatiki Hornby.
  • Minecraft Drop-In: Creative gaming at New Brighton Library on Monday, April 20.
  • Teen Takeover: VR, board games, and crafts for ages 12–24 at Tūranga every Wednesday.
